Patisserie

The pastry in French is called “Patisserie”, it has been recognized since the Middle Ages and keep fascinating the world. The patisserie is a mixed of art and unique taste, traditionnaly light and sweet.
In our shop, we introduce you a list of patisserie from different part of France that French people usually eat in the daily life.

Viennoiserie

The first viennoiserie shop in France was opened by August Zang, who is from Vienna. He brought new idea to the French pastry.
The viennoiserie are the mix between patisserie and French bread, and typically made from puff pastry in order to make the dough flakery. They are typically eaten at breakfast or as snack.
